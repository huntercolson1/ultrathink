Why research systems need changelogs

The longer a system runs, the more invisible adjustments it accumulates. Document each change and the team keeps its calibration.

Every research environment has drift. Without an explicit changelog, drift compounds into confusion. A simple table — timestamp, owner, change, reason — eliminates the mystery. Future you will thank present you for stopping the entropy.
